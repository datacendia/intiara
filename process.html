Creative process

Design comes first. Every INTIARA piece begins as a drawing — then the noble materials are chosen to serve it.

Sketch · SINCHI

It begins with a sketch

From drawing to piece

Before a single thread is woven, each piece is drawn by hand. The sketch fixes the proportion, the geometry and the details — the silver, the tassel, the stone — long before the materials are chosen.

The Andean opal is the hero stone of Collection I. SINCHI's geometric front panel is drawn by hand and then interpreted in noble materials — handwoven, structured and finished in 950 silver.
