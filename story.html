The House

The Vision Behind INTIARA

INTIARA was born from the belief that true luxury never needs to demand attention. It is discovered. In the nobility of exceptional materials. In the dedication of skilled hands. In the respect for time. And in the thoughtful details that transform an object into a meaningful piece.

Each creation brings together contemporary design and some of Peru's finest materials: Baby Suri Alpaca, handwoven textiles, 950 silver and carefully selected semi-precious stones that celebrate the country's natural and cultural heritage.

More than creating handbags, INTIARA creates pieces that invite discovery. The story of a land. Its artisans. Its noble materials. And a vision of elegance where beauty reveals itself gradually.

The founder

Cecilia Yacarini

Behind INTIARA is Peruvian designer and founder Cecilia Yacarini. After a successful professional career in the financial sector, she chose to return to the dream that had accompanied her since her youth: creating a contemporary luxury design house inspired by Peru's extraordinary cultural heritage.

Every collection begins with a different inspiration. The first chapter, Andean Opal, celebrates the light that comes from within through five handbags, each representing one essential feminine quality: serene strength, harmony, authenticity, inner light and fulfilment.

INTIARA does not seek to follow trends. Its purpose is to create timeless pieces that endure beyond seasons and accompany women who value authenticity, craftsmanship and meaningful design.

Because we believe that true beauty never needs to be loud. It simply waits to be discovered.
